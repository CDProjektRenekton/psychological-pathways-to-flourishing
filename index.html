Introduction & Informed Consent
I am Jolina U. Villalobos, a graduate student currently enrolled in the Master of Arts in Psychology program at MAPUA University. As part of the requirements for my degree, I am conducting a study entitled:

"Psychological Pathways Linking Physical Activity and Flourishing: A Moderated Mediation Model through Burnout and Social Support among Filipino Workers."

This study seeks to examine how physical activity relates to flourishing, with particular attention to the mediating role of burnout and the moderating role of social support in the workplace. The findings aim to benefit Filipino workers, organizations, and mental health practitioners.

Purpose of the Study

This research aims to explore the psychological pathways linking physical activity and flourishing, focusing on burnout and social support among Filipino workers.

Procedures

Risks and Benefits
